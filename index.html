Caluculate Your BMR
Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the amount of energy internal body parts like heart, liver, kidney etc. needs to function. Simply put it this way, if a person lies down whole day then BMR would equal the amount of energy utilized.
Caluculate Your TDEE
Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) is the amount of calories utilized whole day. TDEE = BMR + Amount of energy used from walking, standing, excercise etc.

The goal is to eat more than BMR but less than TDEE inorder to lose weight.
